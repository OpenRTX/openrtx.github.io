# Radtel RT-4D

## Specifications
* MCU: AT32F423CCT7
* DMR Chip: HF6853
* FM Broadcast Receiver Chip: BK1080
* Radio Chip: BK4819
* SPI Flash: PY25Q128HA 16MiB
* Display: 128x64 monochrome TFT
* 0x2800 Byte Bootloader
